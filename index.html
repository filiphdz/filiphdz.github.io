Filip Hajdar Drnovšek Zorko is a Slovenian-born writer and translator. He grew up in Slovenia, Ireland, Australia, and the UK, and currently resides just outside Portland, Maine. He understands that his name is a bit confusing, and would like you to know that "Drnovšek Zorko" is the surname. He attended Clarion West in 2019. In his spare time he is a keen quizzer—British readers may recognise him from that one time he was on University Challenge. Follow him on Bluesky @filiphdz.bsky.social

He is represented by Joshua Bilmes at JABberwocky Literary Agency.
